Challenges and Solutions

Challenge: Erosion and Coastal Degradation

Coastal erosion threatens the stability of the castle. Rising sea levels and increased storm surges are accelerating the problem.

Solutions:

  • Implement coastal protection measures (sea walls, breakwaters).
  • Promote sustainable tourism practices to reduce environmental impact.
  • Invest in research to understand and mitigate the effects of climate change.

Challenge: Maintaining Historical Integrity

Balancing preservation with tourism and modernization is a constant challenge.

Solutions:

  • Develop a comprehensive preservation plan.
  • Use non-invasive techniques for restoration.
  • Engage local communities in the preservation process.
