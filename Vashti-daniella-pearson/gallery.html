SOME UNPOPULAR TOURIST SITES

Hans Cottage Botel is a unique eco-tourism destination in Ghana's Central Region, near Kakum National Park. It's known for its restaurant and lodging built over a pond inhabited by live crocodiles. Visitors can safely observe the crocodiles from the restaurant or their rooms. It offers a blend of nature, dining, and a slightly thrilling experience.

Biriwa Beach Hotel is a popular resort located in Biriwa, Ghana, known for its direct access to a sandy beach on the Gulf of Guinea. It offers a range of amenities, including swimming pools, restaurants, and comfortable accommodations, making it a favorite for both local and international tourists seeking relaxation and recreation by the sea.
